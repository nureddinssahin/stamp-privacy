1. Introduction

Thank you for using STAMP. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data. By using the app you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account info: Your email and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile info: Name, username and bio
  • Concert records: Your stamps, notes and ratings
  • Usage data: In-app preferences and settings

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide app features (setlist, Wrapped, badges)
  • Personalise your experience
  • Detect and fix technical issues

4. Data Sharing

We never sell or rent your personal data. Some data may be shared with these services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and storage
  • Last.fm / TheAudioDB: Artist images
  • Setlist.fm: Setlist data
  • Ticketmaster: Upcoming events

5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase infrastructure. Passwords are never stored in plain text. Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption is used.

6. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all data, contact us at info@stampapp.online. Requests are processed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

STAMP is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you via the app when significant changes occur.

9. Contact
